Overview

Offering quality accommodation in the heart of Ho Chi Minh City, Blue Diamond Hotel is a popular pick for both business and leisure travelers. This property was opened in January 2010 and honorably named as the Best Hotel in all of Ho Chi Minh City according to Fairways Magazine. The property combines the best of world-class facilities with a top-notch business center and meeting venue along with tasteful guestrooms to please all its guests. At the Blue Diamond Hotel, every effort is made to make guests feel comfortable. All rooms are spacious and modern provided with high quality amenities.

Room & Suites

Superior

Our Superior room is 22 square meters with wooden floor and interior window, no view. Each room includes two single beds or one queen bed with working desk, fashion chair, bathroom with shower or bathtub. Offering a building window with wooden floor, Premium Deluxe room is 34 square meters including one queen bed with IDD telephone, television LCD, tea making facility...

Room Features

Interior window, no view, Air conditioning, Working desk, Hair dryer, Bathtub / Shower, Coffee/tea maker, Complimentary bottled water, Refrigerator, Satellite/cable TV, Internet access – Wi-Fi, Smoking / Non- smoking room.

Children and extra bed policy

The maximum occupancy of Superior room is 02 adults and 01 child under 06 years old.

Child under 6 stays for free when sharing existing bed with parents. Extra bed is not available for this room.

Check-in time: 14:00. Check-out time: 12:00 noon.

Late check-in before 18:00 is equivalent to half day rental. Late check-in after 18:00 is equivalent to full day rental.
