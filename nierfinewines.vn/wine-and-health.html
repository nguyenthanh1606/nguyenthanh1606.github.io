Wine and health

Not only is the premium drink on the banquet table, wine also has unexpected benefits for human health. If used in moderation with the appropriate dosage, you will feel the results after a period of use.
 

For taste
The beautiful red color of red wine stimulates the user's taste buds. In particular, the aroma of fruit and the acrid taste of alcohol when you touch your lips will increase cravings.

The special aroma and ingredients make grape wine a very suitable drink to eat with rice or bread, not only can appetizers, help digest food, improve the quality of meals but also make Let us feel excited, comfortable mentally.

 

For cardiovascular disease
Polyphenol is a component of red wine, which has the effect of making the body less likely to produce bad proteins that cause blood vessel constriction, which helps the blood and oxygen flow can be easily transported to the heart, protects For the heart against atherosclerosis.

However, not everyone with heart disease should actively drink wine. Please consult with your doctor and cardiologist about your condition to make the best choice for your health.

 

For cancer
Red wine also contains resveratol and tannin which are effective in preventing prostate disease and lung cancer.

Resveratol can also help treat obesity, reduce blood fat by absorbing fat from food through the intestines, especially reducing bad cholesterol - good news for people who often lead a low lifestyle. exercise, or sit at a desk a lot ...

 

Helps extend life

In wine, there are also active antioxidants, slow down the aging process of the body, younger, ruddy skin than those who rarely or never use wine.

In addition, wine also helps reduce the risk of depression. Regulate sleep, helps sleep better, because red wine contains metatoin, a substance that acts as a biological clock in your body.

 

There are many benefits of wine for human health still proved by scientists, but with the above points, it is also great for life, right?
