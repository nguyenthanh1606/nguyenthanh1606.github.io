Pago de Cirsus

Reviews: 
Production house: Araex
Grapes: Tempranillo, Shiraz
Alcohol: 14.5 %
Region: Spain
Volume: 750ml
Awards: Sommelier Wine Awards 2015 - Gold Medal

 

 

Pago de Cirsus is a premium premium wine bottle, it is particularly impressive in the country of Spain, produced by Tempranillo and shiraz grapes, grape varieties with a thick crust, and high tannin, deep purple red. The wine has a rich aroma of oak, ripe plum, lavender, a bit of leather, cloves. The strong, tannin-like taste is long lasting, acid, bold, and slightly spicy with a tongue tip, good and long aftertaste, harmonious due to long immersion in new oak barrels. Enjoy the best at temperatures of 16 - 18 ° C. Best served with dishes like steak, garlic toast and so on.

Merus. 4

Reviews: 
Production house: Araex
Grapes: Tempranillo
Alcohol: 14.5 %
Region: Spain
Volume: 750ml
Awards: Mundusvini 2013 - Germany - Gold Medal

 

The taste of Merus.4 Tempranillo is an improvised blend of perfect ripe berries with sour, attractive sweetness of chocolate and a sudden spiciness from mint. Merus.4 has 14.5% of alcohol level, suitable for diners so Merus.4 is often used to serve in big parties or festivals. Just having Merus.4 accompanying delicious dishes, it can be said that the party has already succeeded.

Papale Primitivo

Reviews: 
Production house: Varvaglione
Grapes: Primitivo
Alcohol: 14.5 %
Region: Italy
Volume: 750ml
Awards: CWSA 2019 - Double Gold Medal

 

In 2013, the Vatican used 115 bottles of Papale Primitivo to treat 115 cardinals from all around the world after the election of Pope Francis. Since then, Papale Primitivo is one of the most famous and special wines that are very popular in luxurious and parties, gatherings. This wine has a deep red color, highlighted by the flavor of wild berries and fruit jams. Enjoy the wine feeling smooth and evenly rounded flavor, easy to drink. Best served with dishes made from red meats, chicken.
