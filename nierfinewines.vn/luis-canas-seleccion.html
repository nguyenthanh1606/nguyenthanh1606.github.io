Luis Canas Seleccion

Reviews: 
Production house: Araex
Grapes: Tempranillo, Cabernet Sauvignon
Alcohol: 14.5 %
Region: Spain
Volume: 750ml
Awards: Mundusvini 2015 - Germany - Gold Medal

 

Luis Canas Seleccion de la Familia Reserva is made from Tempranillo grapes (85%) and Chardonay sauvignon (15%). This wine has emerald red color with a cherry blossom color on the rim. With many complex aromas combined together, Luis Canas La Famillia gives us a strong, sophisticated taste. Initially we can feel the taste of ripe berries, the smell of smoke and raisins. After a certain amount of time, the smells of basil and jam begin to rise, and a little more time, the aroma of roasting and spice emerging more clearly. It’s the best wine to drink with grilled dishes, lamb and ham.
