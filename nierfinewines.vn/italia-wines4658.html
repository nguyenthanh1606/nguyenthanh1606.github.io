Privata Primitivo di Maduria

Reviews: 
Production house: Varvaglione
Grapes: Primitivo
Alcohol: 15.5%
Region: Italy
Volume: 750 ml
Awards: Mundus Vini 2019 - Double Gold

 

Privata Primitivo has a deep red color with some purple. This wine has an attractive flavor with a hint of aroma reminiscent of red fruit jam. To produce a scrumptious bottle of premium Privata Primitivo, the Primitivo grape must be steeped with controlled temperature at 26°C - 28°C. After malolactic fermentation, refinement in French barrels and American barriques at least for 14 months. Privata Primitivo perfectly served with dishes made from red meats, chicken and aged cheese.

Patrisso Primitivo di Manduria

Reviews: 
Production house: Varvaglione
Grapes: Primitivo
Alcohol: 18.5 %
Region: Italy
Volume: 750ml

 

Patrisso Primitivo di Manduria is a special wine made from the very good and popular Primitivo grape. Using special techniques of indigenous people, with concentrated grape juice, wine soaked in oak barrels for 18 months, 12 months in bottles before being out to market. The wine has a strikingly beautiful red color, with a rich aroma of ripe plums, fruit, cherry, coffee, and aromatic oak, longer than leather, clove. Patrisso Primitivo has a balanced and harmonious structure, and a long aftertaste. This wine is best enjoyed at temperatures of 16 - 18 ° C. Perfect when drinking with red meats, cheese.

1921 Primitivo di manduria

Reviews: 
Production house: Varvaglione
Grapes: Primitivo
Alcohol: 14.5 %
Region: Italy
Volume: 750ml

 

1921 Primitivo di manduria is a deep red with a hint of violet. The wine has a light aroma with notes of red fruits, dried figs and black cherries surrounded by elegant vanilla. The smooth and elegant wine flavor with fruity notes is relieved by the sweet notes of cloves and black pepper. The wine was aged in large wooden casks for 18 months at temperatures of 26°C and continued for 9 months in stainless steel tanks. Perfect when drinking with red meats, chicken.
