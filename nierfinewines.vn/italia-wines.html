12 E Mezzo Malvasia

Reviews: 
Production house: Varvaglione
Grapes: Malvasia Bianca
Alcohol: 12.5 %
Region: Italy
Volume: 750ml
Awards: Concours Mondial Bruxelles 2019 – Gold

 

The first sensation when coming to 12 E Mezzo Malvasia is the strong fruity aroma at the nose tip, strong and intense as the taste of spring. Beautiful colour with the blend of Colore Golden and straw-yellow wine, the palate is overwhelmed by its fresh liveliness and balance by a harmonious structure, preserving the delicate taste in the aftertaste. A genuine white Mezzo bottle is made from Malvasia grape, soaked for 24 hours, then softened, fermented at 14°C and brewed in stainless steel. And there's nothing better than enjoying 12 E Mezzo Malvasia with dishes made from fish, clams, shellfish, cheese.

12 E Mezzo Primitivo

Reviews: 
Production house: Varvaglione
Grapes: Primitivo
Alcohol: 12.5 %
Region: Italy
Volume: 750ml
Awards: CWSA 2019 - Double Gold Medal

 

The seductive scent of ripe red plums, cherry blossom jam and a bit of an attractive aftertaste from vanilla, cocoa is a special feature when the wine is opened. Along with that, the smooth and delicate taste of the ripe tannins made this wine even more attractive. Vinification by maceration with controlled temperature at 24°- 28°C. After malolactic fermentation, refinement in American barrique at least for 3 – 6 months. Best to drink with aged cheese, pasta and pizza.

12 E Mezzo (Organic)

Reviews: 
Production house: Varvaglione
Grapes: Primitivo
Alcohol: 12.5 %
Region: Italy
Volume: 750ml
Awards: Concours Mondial Bruxelles 2019 - Silver

 

12 E Mezzo Primitivo (Organic) mysterious with intense red, with violet hints reminiscent of fresh red fruit. The first pervasive fragrance when opening the wine is the fruity notes of currants and blackberries, elegantly join an aromatic bouquet of nutmeg, liquorice, chocolate and vanilla. Its low alcoholic degree is supported by a slightly acid structure that makes it balanced and long, leaving the palate full of coconut and fruity notes. Pasta, white meat and fresh cheese are perfect choices when served with this wine.

Privata

Reviews: 
Production house: Varvaglione
Grapes: Negro Amaro
Alcohol: 15 %
Region: Italy
Volume: 750ml
Awards: Berliner Wein Trophy 2019 - Gold

 

This wine is distinguished by the elegant connection between the notes of red fruits, black pepper, cinnamon, tobacco and the special aroma of licorice. On the palate, its balance leaves a long and deep balsamic aroma. To produce such a delicious bottle of premium Privata Negroamaro, vinification by maceration with controlled temperature at 24°-26°C. After malolactic fermentation, refinement in French barrels and American tonneau at least for 6 months. Perfect when drinking with dishes made from red meats, pasta and cheese.

Papale Primitivo

Reviews: 
Production house: Varvaglione
Grapes: Primitivo
Alcohol: 14.5 %
Region: Italy
Volume: 750ml
Awards: CWSA 2019 - Double Gold Medal

 

In 2013, the Vatican used 115 bottles of Papale Primitivo to treat 115 cardinals from all around the world after the election of Pope Francis. Since then, Papale Primitivo is one of the most famous and special wines that are very popular in luxurious and parties, gatherings. This wine has a deep red color, highlighted by the flavor of wild berries and fruit jams. Enjoy the wine feeling smooth and evenly rounded flavor, easy to drink. Best served with dishes made from red meats, chicken.
