V6 Negroamaro

Reviews: 
Production house: Varvaglione
Grapes: Negro Amaro
Alcohol: 14 %
Region: Italy
Volume: 750ml

 

V6 Salice Salentino is made from carefully selected grapes of the Salice Salentino region. This wine has a dark red color, the taste of ripe red fruits, raisin flavor, a little spicy pepper, soft tannin, faintly scrumptious cigar smell. Deep and long aftertaste. V6 Wine Salice Salentino is best served with red meats, lamb, beef, grilled ribs and soft cheese. Enjoy the wine at a temperature of 15 - 17 ° C, should pour the wine into a decanter for at least 20 minutes before enjoying.
