Pago de Cirsus

Reviews: 
Production house: Araex
Grapes: Tempranillo, Shiraz
Alcohol: 14.5 %
Region: Spain
Volume: 750ml
Awards: Sommelier Wine Awards 2015 - Gold Medal

 

 

Pago de Cirsus is a premium premium wine bottle, it is particularly impressive in the country of Spain, produced by Tempranillo and shiraz grapes, grape varieties with a thick crust, and high tannin, deep purple red. The wine has a rich aroma of oak, ripe plum, lavender, a bit of leather, cloves. The strong, tannin-like taste is long lasting, acid, bold, and slightly spicy with a tongue tip, good and long aftertaste, harmonious due to long immersion in new oak barrels. Enjoy the best at temperatures of 16 - 18 ° C. Best served with dishes like steak, garlic toast and so on.

Pago de Cirsus Oak

Reviews: 
Production house: Araex
Grapes: Tempranillo
Alcohol: 14.5 %
Region: Spain
Volume: 750ml
Awards: Sommelier Wine Awards 2015 - Commended

 

Pago Oak is a deep red with a hint of blue, the scent of ripe blueberries emphasized by the notes of firewood and dry straw. Pago Oak has a very strong but not heavy, showing the solid structure, sweet aftertaste of tannins. To produce a bottle of Pago Oak, Tempranillo grapes are tested, then soft-press, refrigerated for four days at 8°C before undergoing fermentation in a stainless steel tanks at a controlled temperature. Best served with ravioli cheese and beef pasta pasta.

Artero Tempranillo

Reviews: 
Production house: Araex
Grapes: Tempranillo
Alcohol: 14 %
Region: Spain
Volume: 750ml
Awards: Mundusvini 2014 - Germany - Gold Medal

 

Artero Reserva has a deep cherry color with a hint of red on the surface of the wine. When opened, the wine gives off a harmonious aroma of plums, grapes, bay leaf and eucalyptus. Smooth wine flavor, with ripe fruit flavors, coffee notes, a hint of vanilla and basil. To produce a premium bottle of Artero Reserva, Tempranillo grapes must be ripe, hand-picked, soft-pressed and pre-processed for 24 hours at 15°C. It is then fermented and incubated for 10 days at 25°C. The wine aged for 12 months in American oak and 225 liter oak barrels before being sorted and bottled. Artero Reserva is perfect when served with grilled meat and stews, or cooked beans and cheese.

Villa Conchi Seleccion

Reviews: 
Production house: Araex
Grapes: Xarel.lo, Parellada, Macabeo, Chardonnay
Alcohol: 11.5 %
Region: Spain
Volume: 750ml
Awards: Berliner 2015 - Germany - Silver Medal

 

This wine has a vibrant green and yellow color, exudes a passionate fruity aroma, has a fresh, warm, pleasant and round flavor with a long aftertaste. To produce a bottle of Villa Conchi Cava Brut Seleccion, grapes must be harvested at night to avoid high temperatures and early fermentation. It is then quickly transported to the factory and refrigerated at 10°C, further undergoing soft pressing, fermentation at 16°C. The next brewing time, the wine is distilled in a cellar at 15 - 17°C. This sparkling wine is perfect as an appetizer as well as an accessory for salads, rice and light fish dishes.

Villa Conchi Rose

Reviews: 
Production house: Araex
Grapes: Trepat
Alcohol: 11.5 %
Region: Spain
Volume: 750ml
Awards: Mundusvini 2015 - Germany - Gold Medal

 

This wine has an extremely attractive cherry red color with continuous bubbles when opened, a medium fruity aroma on the nose and a fresh, pleasant taste with a fine and soft texture in the palate. To produce a bottle of Villa Conchi Cava Brut Rose, grapes must be harvested at night to avoid high temperatures and early fermentation. They are quickly transported to a winery and refrigerated at 15 ° C. Flowers must be harvested by pressing the grapes lightly, so it does not contain tannic materials that confer sourness to the wine. This sparkling wine is perfect as an appetizer as well as an accessory for salads, rice, seafood and light fish dishes.

Luis Crianza

Reviews: 
Production house: Araex
Grapes: Tempranillo, Garnacha
Alcohol: 14 %
Region: Spain
Volume: 750ml
Awards: Mundusvini 2015 - Germany - Gold Medal

 

Luis Canas Crianza has a jade red color, an aromatic aftertaste of oak. Very soft in the palate and round with a raspberry-like cherry fruit flavor ... With a pleasant finish along with the reappearance of the aromatic aftertaste. Ideal with meat paellas, roasted meat and poultry, chorizo and other Spanish cured meats. Luis Canas wine won a gold medal at Mundus Vini 2015 and made from the 30-year old Tempranillo and Garnacha grapes. After its primary fermentation, the wine is placed in American and French oak barrels where it undergoes malolactic fermentation and is aged for a minimum of 12 months. It is then bottled an aged for at least another 12 months.

Luis Reserva

Reviews: 
Production house: Araex
Grapes: Tempranillo, Graciano
Alcohol: 14.5 %
Region: Spain
Volume: 750ml
Awards: Mundusvini 2015 - Germany - Gold medal

 

Luis Canas Reserva is a deep cherry-red wine with a medium-high depth. When first opened, it has the aromas of black fruit and fruit compote, spices, baked goods and dairy products with a long retro nasal sensation, that lasts on the palate. Elegant and silky. Best served with grilled meats, fish and cheese. Luis Canas Reserva is extremely popular in Rioja cuisine - famous place in Spain when served with dishes such as chili marinated with codfish, artichokes with ham and migor pastoriles.

Angeles

Reviews: 
Production house: Araex
​​​ Grapes: Tempranillo, Graciano
Alcohol: 14 %
Region: Spain
Volume: 750ml

Angeles de Amaren wines have a strong aroma with a clear fruit content on the base of the oak aroma, possessing lactic pigments and spicy flavors when drinking quickly.
